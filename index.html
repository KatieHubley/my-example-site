About Open Educational Resources

OERs have saved students millions on course materials cost, and have helped higher educational institutions as well. According to the report from the Open Educational Resources Degree Initiative from Achieving the Dream, students enrolled in OER courses earned more credits than those taking non-OER courses. Additionally, Inside Higher Ed reports researchers have found “an average of $1.03 in gross revenue generated for every dollar spent on redesigning courses [as OERs].”

OERs have increased access to free resources for student learning. Here you’ll find practical guides for designing learning modules for your content. Of course, all of the resources here are free for use. OERs for OERs!
